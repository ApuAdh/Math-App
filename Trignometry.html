Trignometry

Triangles

Pythagorean theorem: a²+b²=c²

Sin(0)=Opposite/Hypotenuse, eg: Sin(A)=b/c

Cos(0)=Adjacent/Hypotenuse, eg: Cos(A)=a/c

Tan(0)=Opposite/Adjacent, eg: Tan(A)=b/a

Csc(0)=Hypotenuse/Opposite, eg: Csc(A)=c/b

Cot(0)=Adjacent/Opposite, eg: Cot(A)=a/b

Sec(0)=Hypotenuse/Adjacent, eg: Sec(A)=c/a

Circles

Sin & Cosine Law:

c²=a²+b²-2ab cos(C)

a²=b²+c²-2bc cos(A)

b²=a²+c²-2ac cos(B)
